## Introduction 

My name is Nikhil Navaratna and I am currently working towards my Master's thesis in TIFR Hyderabad under the supervison of Prof. M. Krishnamurthy. I work on devising methods to use hot electrons generated due to intense laser-matter interactions as electron sources for Transmission Electron Microscopy. 

This blog will contain multiple sections. Out of the loop which will try and explain latest trends in light-matter interaction and quantum optics in layman's terms. Explained, which will contain blog articles which elucidate the concepts of Physics in a more fundamental manner. Experimental, which will deal with all the crazy experiments I do. Research which will contain more about my work and interests. It will also have a section dedicated to the books, podcasts and youtube channels I am watching/reading. Exsimulate which will primarily be on using simulations to understand various concepts, sort of like tutorials.
